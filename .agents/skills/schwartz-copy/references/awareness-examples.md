# Awareness Stage Examples

Detailed copy examples for each stage of awareness across digital channels.

## Stage 1: Unaware

The prospect doesn't know they have a problem. You must create awareness before you can sell.

### Facebook Ad (Top of Funnel)

**Bad approach:** "Try our sleep supplement—50% off!"
*Why it fails: They don't know they have a sleep problem.*

**Good approach:**
> Ever notice how some people seem to have endless energy while you're running on fumes by 2pm?
>
> It's not about willpower. It's about something happening during the 3rd hour of your sleep cycle.
>
> [Watch the 2-minute explainer]

### Blog Post Headline

- "The Hidden Cost of 'Good Enough' Sleep"
- "Why High Performers Are Obsessed With This One Metric"
- "I Tracked My Energy for 30 Days. Here's What Surprised Me."

### Landing Page Structure

1. **Hook:** Curiosity or story that hints at a hidden problem
2. **Revelation:** Explain the problem they didn't know they had
3. **Agitation:** Show the consequences of ignoring it
4. **Solution intro:** Gently introduce solution category (not your product yet)
5. **CTA:** Soft ask—"Get the free guide" / "Watch the video"

## Stage 2: Problem-Aware

They know something's wrong but don't know what to do about it.

### Facebook Ad

**Hook:** Start with the problem they're actively feeling.

> Sick of staring at the ceiling at 3am?
>
> You've tried melatonin. You've tried the sleep apps. You've tried keeping your phone out of the bedroom.
>
> Nothing works for more than a week.
>
> The reason isn't what you think. [See why →]

### Google Search Ad

*Targeting: "can't sleep at night" / "insomnia help"*

> Stop Battling Insomnia
> The real reason you can't sleep isn't stress—it's your circadian rhythm. 
> Free assessment shows your sleep type.

### Email Subject Lines

- "Why everything you've tried for sleep hasn't worked"
- "The 3am problem (and what's actually causing it)"
- "You're not lazy. You're sleep-deprived."

### Landing Page Structure

1. **Problem headline:** Name the pain directly
2. **Validation:** "If you've tried X, Y, Z and still struggle..."
3. **The real cause:** Explain what's actually going on
4. **Solution teaser:** Introduce the type of solution that works
5. **CTA:** "Get the free sleep assessment" / "Download the guide"

---

## Stage 3: Solution-Aware

They know solutions exist and want results—but don't know about your specific product.

### Facebook Ad

**Hook:** Lead with the outcome, introduce your mechanism.

> Fall asleep in under 15 minutes. Every night.
>
> The Sleep Reset Protocol uses chronotype matching to align your body's natural rhythm with your schedule.
>
> No pills. No gadgets. Just science.
>
> [Start your free trial]

### Google Search Ad

*Targeting: "best sleep program" / "how to fix sleep schedule"*

> Fall Asleep in 15 Minutes Flat
> The Sleep Reset Protocol—personalized to your chronotype. 
> 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

### Landing Page Structure

1. **Outcome headline:** Promise the result they want
2. **Mechanism intro:** Explain your unique approach (the "how")
3. **Differentiation:** Why this works when others don't
4. **Proof:** Testimonials, data, before/after
5. **Offer:** Clear product description + pricing
6. **CTA:** "Start free trial" / "Get started"

### Email Sequence (3-part)

**Email 1:** Introduce the mechanism
> "Most sleep advice treats symptoms. Here's why we focus on your chronotype instead..."

**Email 2:** Proof and credibility
> "Sarah went from 4 hours of broken sleep to 7+ hours in 12 days. Here's her story..."

**Email 3:** Soft close
> "Ready to try the Sleep Reset Protocol? Your first 14 days are free."

## Stage 4: Product-Aware

They know about your product but need convincing that it's right for them.

### Retargeting Ad

**Hook:** Address objections, stack proof.

> Still thinking about Sleep Reset?
>
> Here's what 3,000+ users said after their first week:
> ✓ 89% fell asleep faster
> ✓ 73% reported more energy during the day
> ✓ Average: 47 more minutes of sleep per night
>
> Try it free for 14 days. No risk.

### Email (Objection Handler)

**Subject:** "Is Sleep Reset right for you? (honest answer)"

> You might be wondering if this will work for your specific situation.
>
> Let me be direct: Sleep Reset isn't for everyone.
>
> It's NOT for you if:
> - You have diagnosed sleep apnea (see a doctor first)
> - You're looking for a quick pill-based fix
> - You're not willing to adjust your routine for 2 weeks
>
> But if you're dealing with garden-variety insomnia, stress-related sleep issues, or an inconsistent schedule—this was built for you.
>
> [See if you qualify →]

### Landing Page Additions

At this stage, your landing page should emphasize:
- **Comparison charts** (vs. competitors or alternatives)
- **Case studies** with specific results
- **FAQ section** addressing common objections
- **Guarantee details** prominently displayed
- **Trust badges** and security assurances

## Stage 5: Most Aware

They want it. Just give them a reason to act now.

### Retargeting Ad

> Your Sleep Reset trial is waiting.
>
> Start tonight. Cancel anytime.
>
> [Activate Free Trial]

### Cart Abandonment Email

**Subject:** "Your Sleep Reset is waiting"

> You're one click away from better sleep.
>
> Your personalized Sleep Reset Protocol is ready. Complete your signup in 30 seconds.
>
> [Complete Setup →]
>
> P.S. Not sure? Remember: 14 days free, cancel anytime, no questions asked.

### Limited-Time Offer Email

**Subject:** "48 hours: Sleep Reset at 40% off"

> Quick note: We're running a 48-hour sale.
>
> Sleep Reset: $29/month (normally $49)
>
> This is the lowest price we've offered this year.
>
> [Lock in 40% off →]
>
> Offer expires Friday at midnight.

### Checkout Page Copy

Keep it minimal. Don't re-sell. Just reassure:
- "30-day money-back guarantee"
- "Cancel anytime"
- "Join 12,000+ members"
- "Secure checkout"
