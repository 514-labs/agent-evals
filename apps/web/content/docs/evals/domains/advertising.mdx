title: "Advertising"
description: "Data warehousing patterns for ad delivery, performance, and attribution analytics."
---

Advertising domains process impression, click, conversion, and bid streams under strict latency and throughput constraints.

## Core Data

- Impression and click events from delivery infrastructure.
- Conversion events with attribution metadata.
- Bid requests, responses, and auction outcomes.
- Campaign, creative, audience, and placement dimensions.

## Characteristic Challenges

- Extreme write throughput requires efficient ingestion and storage design.
- Real-time aggregation is needed for pacing, spend, and performance monitoring.
- Attribution windows and identity resolution complicate conversion credit.
- Fraud filtering and invalid traffic handling must not distort KPIs.

## Evaluation Focus

- Build high-throughput ingestion and aggregation pipelines.
- Model campaign performance metrics with clear attribution rules.
- Support near-real-time reporting for pacing and optimization workflows.
- Maintain analytical correctness under late events and data corrections.

## Typical Outputs

- Spend, impressions, clicks, and conversions by campaign and time bucket.
- CTR, CVR, and CPA trends by audience segment.
- Real-time pacing dashboards against budget targets.
- Attribution reports by channel, creative, and conversion window.
